Our Story

Meet Gurpreet

Bake Bliss began in a small home kitchen in Khargone with one simple belief: dessert should feel like a celebration in itself. What started as baking for friends and family soon turned into a bakery loved across the city.

Every cake that leaves our kitchen is made from scratch — no mixes, no shortcuts — just quality ingredients, careful technique, and an eye for detail that comes from genuinely loving what I do.

Today, Bake Bliss is proud to be rated 5.0★ by our customers, and every single order still gets the same personal attention as the very first one.
