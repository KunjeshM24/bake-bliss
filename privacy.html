Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

At Bake Bliss by Gurpreet ("we," "our," "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or place an order.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide via our enquiry form (name, phone number, email address).
  • Order details such as cake preferences, delivery address, and event date.
  • Basic usage data such as pages visited, collected through standard analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries and process orders.
  • To communicate updates about your order via phone, WhatsApp, or email.
  • To improve our website and customer experience.
  • To send occasional newsletters, only if you opt in.

3. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and remember preferences such as theme selection. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only with delivery partners strictly for the purpose of fulfilling your order.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at hello@bakeblissbygurpreet.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via email or WhatsApp.
